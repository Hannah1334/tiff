Heart Points (HP)
This is a method of indicating the strength of relationship between a member of the Troop and the Merchant in question. Heart Points are earned at random throughout the story when appropriate, development must happen between the Merchant and the Player Character in order for these specific kinds of points to be acquired. How does this translate into their Merchant Mechanics? When purchasing pitched equipment from a Merchant their Heart Points act as a coupon, with each individual heart being a passive constant reduction to the overall price of the equipment they want to purchase. Each individual heart is worth a 10% discount, and they stack, so if Max Relationship is achieved with a Merchant NPC that means your Player Character will always have a 50% off discount from their prices! Keep in mind that Heart Points are never set in stone, if the Merchant’s opinion of the Player Character changes drastically enough Heart Points CAN BE LOST.

Bounty Points (BP)
Whenever Troopers get to set up camp (while one or both of the Merchant NPCs are also staying in the same camp) a Bounty Bulletin will be made available. Bounties are essentially tasks designated--and signed by--each individual Merchant. Completing the Bounties will reward the Player Character with Bounty Points, which can be used to purchase items, single use spells, or equipment from the Merchants who assigned the task. BP earned from one Merchant cannot be used on purchases for another. Once earned, these points cannot be lost, they exist only to be spent on their respective Merchant, and are strictly an O.O.C. concept (similar to the popular Magic School point earning systems).

How To Use
At any point in time a member of the Rowan Troop can place a special order to any of the available Merchant NPCs. This order and/or request must be within their skillset, but can be literally anything achievable by either (or both) of the Merchant’s own Magic Trees. Equipment will be pitched to myself (Hannah/Fae/Being) via Private Messages, and will go through an approval process as I’m sure you’re all used to by now. During this process the pitch will receive a point cost, which can only be paid off with Bounty Points (BP). Discounts from Heart Points (HP) will automatically be factored into the quoted price. The Player Character will not receive the equipment they ordered until they have earned the necessary BP required to pay for the pitch.
Faith
"Faith mages are a completely support-oriented group of mages. Those who practice Faith hold the ability to mend wounds, lift curses and illnesses, bestow temporary magical boons to their allies, or create Holy Equipment which provide a permanent boon. Faith mages are also highly skilled in defensive arts, and are capable of erecting magical barriers. However, faith mana is completely non hostile, and no faith spell can ever cause harm to another being (unless faith magic specifically harms them)."

Spiritualism
"Arguably one of the most powerful trees; Spiritualists possess the ability to speak to and beseech the spirits around themselves. This often requires much time, and also requires the spirits to agree with the mage's cause. One without conviction will not do well within this study. Spiritualists gain access to a unique mana type at Mage rank, which has been deemed "Spirit Force." Spirit Force has the capability to heal the soul, and at some level the flesh as well.​"
